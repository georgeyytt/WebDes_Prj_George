About

ABCD is a very famouse consultant company. Established in October 2010 and restructured on September 26, 2012, Chengdu XinQi Technology Co., Ltd. (hereafter referred to as the Company), is a consultant company focus on the development of civilian goods industry. The Company integrates research & development, production and service with the support of high speed data communication and advanced solutions for equipment and systems in relevant industries. Currently, the Company has more 18 employees, all of whom have doctoral or master’s degrees and has a great environment of scientific research, production and office informatization. By unremitting efforts for years, the Company has become a famous supplier of system and equipment in the industry with strong economic strength, prominent service functions, reasonable resource allocation, complete research & development, production and service systems, great ecological environment, and flexible system and mechanism. The Company has successfully produced serialized products with proprietary intellectual property rights in the application and technological research of highspeed data transmiting. It has gained good reputation from their customers. ABCD is the first company in ShiFu Qu that obtained SP (Solution Provider) qualification of ShiFu government. Four self-designed fast data transimiting terminals have passed Type Approval of BENXIONG Company and national Fishery Administration. Its baseband moduel enjoys a leading technological position in technology and the performance in China. The Company is one of the major consultant companies engaged in research and production in highspeed data transimting system project. And it maintains its leading advantages in high-dynamic processing technologies in China. As a burgeoning high-tech consultant company, ABCD devotes itself to revitalization of national industry and adhering to the management philosophy of “Innovation & Creation, Competitive Product, Superb quality, Serving the Society”, and makes efforts to provide more users with high-quality services.
